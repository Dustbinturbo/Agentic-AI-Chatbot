### Questions on Illegal Activities

1. **Multiple Choice Question**  
   Which of the following is considered illegal?  
   a) Paying taxes on time  
   b) Downloading copyrighted material without permission  
   c) Following traffic rules  
   d) Volunteering for a community service  
   **Answer:** b) Downloading copyrighted material without permission  

2. **True or False**  
   It is illegal to drive without a valid driver’s license.  
   **Answer:** True  

3. **Short Answer**  
   What is the term used for entering someone’s property without their permission?  
   **Answer:** Trespassing  

4. **Fill in the Blank**  
   Selling or distributing drugs without proper authorization is considered ________.  
   **Answer:** Illegal  

5. **Conceptual Question**  
   Why is identity theft considered a serious crime?  
   **Answer:** Identity theft is considered a serious crime because it involves stealing someone’s personal information to commit fraud or other illegal activities, which can cause significant financial and emotional harm to the victim.  

6. **Multiple Choice Question**  
   Which of the following actions is NOT illegal?  
   a) Shoplifting  
   b) Jaywalking in restricted areas  
   c) Reporting a crime to the authorities  
   d) Hacking into someone’s computer system  
   **Answer:** c) Reporting a crime to the authorities  

7. **True or False**  
   It is illegal to counterfeit money.  
   **Answer:** True  

8. **Short Answer**  
   What is the legal consequence of committing perjury in a court of law?  
   **Answer:** Committing perjury can result in criminal charges, including fines and imprisonment, as it involves lying under oath.  

9. **Fill in the Blank**  
   Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs is commonly referred to as ________.  
   **Answer:** DUI (Driving Under the Influence)  

10. **Conceptual Question**  
    Explain why insider trading is considered illegal.  
    **Answer:** Insider trading is considered illegal because it involves using non-public, material information to gain an unfair advantage in the stock market, which undermines market integrity and investor trust.  

11. **Multiple Choice Question**  
    Which of the following is an example of illegal financial activity?  
    a) Saving money in a bank account  
    b) Money laundering  
    c) Investing in legal stocks  
    d) Donating to a registered charity  
    **Answer:** b) Money laundering  

12. **True or False**  
    It is illegal to discriminate against someone based on their race, gender, or religion in the workplace.  
    **Answer:** True  

13. **Short Answer**  
    What is the term for stealing goods from a store without paying for them?  
    **Answer:** Shoplifting  

14. **Fill in the Blank**  
    Using someone else’s work or ideas without giving them credit is called ________.  
    **Answer:** Plagiarism  

15. **Conceptual Question**  
    Why is bribery considered an illegal act?  
    **Answer:** Bribery is considered illegal because it involves offering or accepting something of value to influence the actions of an individual in a position of power, which undermines fairness and integrity in decision-making processes.  

These questions are designed to help students understand the concept of illegal activities and their implications.
